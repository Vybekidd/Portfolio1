PLANTAIN AND EGG RECIPIE

HOW TO MAKE PLANTAIN AND EGG

Ingridients needed

  • A bunch of plantain
  • 3 Eggs
  • Groundnut oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • boulion cube to taste
  • Ground pepper


    • FRYING THE PLANTAIN
    1. Pour enough groundnut oil on fire
    2. Peel the plaintain
    3. Slice the plaintain
    4. Put the sliced plantains on fire
    5. Bring them out when they are golden brown


    FRYING THE EGG
    1. Crack the eggs into a bowl
    2. Pour enough groundnut oil on fire
    3. Add your salt, boullion and ground pepper as desired then whisk
    4. After enough whisking, pour the whisked egg into the frying pan
    5. When done, pour out the fried eggs into a bowl
